The Cloud That Couldn't Rain

A tale of patience and hope

Clara was a little cloud who wanted nothing more than to rain and help the flowers grow. But no matter how hard she tried, not a single drop would fall.

She watched other clouds shower the earth and felt left out. "Why can't I rain?" she wondered. The sun smiled down and said, "Every cloud has its time, Clara. Be patient."

One day, Clara felt a tickle inside. Suddenly, tiny raindrops began to fall! She giggled with joy as she watered the thirsty earth below. Clara learned that sometimes, good things take time—and when the moment is right, you'll shine (or rain) in your own special way.

The end.
