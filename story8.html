Zara the Zebra's Stripes

A colorful tale of self-love

Zara was a young zebra who wished her stripes were as bright as the rainbow. She tried rolling in flowers and splashing in puddles, but her stripes always stayed black and white.

One day, a wise old giraffe told her, "Your stripes are beautiful just the way they are. They make you unique!" Zara looked at her reflection and realized her stripes were perfect for her.

From that day on, Zara wore her stripes with pride, knowing she was special just the way she was.

The end.
