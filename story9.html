Leo's Lost Yawn

A sleepy safari search

Leo the lion cub couldn't fall asleep—he had lost his yawn! He searched the savanna, asking every animal if they'd seen it.

The wise owl suggested, "Try relaxing and counting stars." As Leo gazed at the twinkling sky, a big yawn finally escaped. He snuggled into his cozy den, happy to have found his sleepiness again.

Sometimes, all you need is a little patience and a peaceful moment to find your way to dreamland.

The end.
