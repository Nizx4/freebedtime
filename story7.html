The Pillow Who Snored

A silly story of sleepy sounds

Puffy the Pillow was the comfiest pillow in the land—but he had a problem. Every night, as soon as someone laid their head on him, he would start to snore! The snores were so silly and soft that they made everyone giggle.

One night, Puffy's snores turned into a gentle lullaby, helping everyone fall asleep faster. Puffy realized that his snoring wasn't a problem at all—it was his special way of spreading sweet dreams.

From then on, Puffy snored proudly, and everyone in the house looked forward to bedtime with a smile.

The end.
