Benny the Blanket

A cozy adventure of comfort

Benny was the softest, fluffiest blanket in the whole house. Every night, he loved to wrap around children and keep them warm. But Benny had a secret wish: he wanted to go on an adventure beyond the bed!

One windy night, Benny caught a breeze and floated out the window. He soared over rooftops, wrapped around a chilly kitten, and even became a superhero's cape for a little while. But soon, Benny missed his favorite place—snuggling sleepy children.

So he drifted back home, just in time for bedtime. Benny realized that the best adventure was bringing comfort and dreams to those he loved most.

The end.
